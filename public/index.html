How it actually works

A personal RAG database and semantic search engine.
Built and controlled from AI chat.

RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation. It is the architecture that lets AI answer questions using your specific knowledge rather than its training data alone. Until now, building one required an engineer, a vector database, an embedding pipeline, and a weekend. MyAITwin gives you all of that from a chat message. Every note you store is embedded and indexed in Pinecone, a production-grade vector database.

Semantic search

Ask "how do I approach difficult conversations?" and it finds your relevant notes even if you never used those exact words. It understands intent, not just text.

Keyword search finds what you typed. Semantic search finds what you meant.

Built from chat

No dashboards. No upload flows. No configuration. Your RAG database grows every time you share something worth keeping in conversation.

Powered by Pinecone. Production infrastructure. Zero setup.

You're the architect

Define your own knowledge types. Add your own tags. Edit anything as your thinking evolves. Delete anything that no longer holds. The structure reflects how you think, not how someone else decided you should organise.
