Security and your data

This is yours. Not ours.

We built MyAITwin so your knowledge is yours. Not a training set. Not a marketing list. Not something we hold over you. Here is exactly how that works.

Isolated

Your own tenant

Every user gets a separate tenant. Your knowledge lives in its own row space and its own vector namespace. Nothing crosses between users, ever. We tested this on every tool with a 35-check isolation suite.

Exportable

Full export, any time

Your data is yours. Ask and we will hand you everything you have stored, in plain JSON. Delete your account and every row, every vector, every token is wiped within seconds.

Not shared

Never used for anything else

We do not train models on your knowledge. We do not share it with third parties. We do not look at it. The only thing we touch is the SHA-256 hash of your token, to verify you're you.
